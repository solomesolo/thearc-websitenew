1) The Concept

Modern health is noisy. Devices and dashboards proliferate while clarity declines. The Arc takes the opposite approach: one focus per month, three daily micro-actions, and periodic reflections. The aim is not perfection but rhythm—a stable pattern your biology can trust.

Each phase teaches why an action matters, not just what to do, so you can self-adjust when life changes. This is a prevention-first framework: small, repeated behaviours that reduce friction in sleep, glucose, inflammation, and stress signalling. The result is sustained energy, steadier mood, and quieter physiology—foundations of long healthspan.
